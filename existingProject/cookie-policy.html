Cookie Policy

Learn how we use cookies to enhance your banking experience.

Last Updated: March 15, 2024

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by enabling certain features and functionality.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for various purposes, including:

  • Essential functionality and security
  • Remembering your preferences
  • Analyzing website performance
  • Personalizing your experience
  • Improving our services

Types of Cookies We Use

Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Required for basic website functionality and security Session
Preference Cookies Remember your settings and preferences 1 year
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how visitors use our website 2 years
Marketing Cookies Track your browsing habits for personalized content 1 year

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control and manage cookies in various ways:

  • Browser settings: Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings
  • Cookie consent: We provide options to accept or decline non-essential cookies
  • Third-party tools: Various tools are available to manage cookie preferences

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages. These third parties may use cookies to:

  • Analyze website traffic
  • Provide social media features
  • Deliver targeted advertising
  • Measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
